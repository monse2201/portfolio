Data, growth & CX for a pet e-commerce brand
tobipets.com · Sales, marketing, retention

tobipets.com — pet e-commerce connecting food, medicine and accessories with home delivery and recurring orders.
“Doggy Box” campaign in collaboration with McDonald's, combining brand awareness and digital growth.
On-site brand activation, connecting offline experiences with online journeys and repeat purchases.

About tobipets.com — pet e-commerce focused on food, medicine and accessories, with a strong emphasis on repeat purchases and AutoShip-style subscriptions.

Role — brought together analytics, growth and customer experience to make decisions easier week by week.

Data & Business Intelligence

  • Structured sales, cohort and retention data to understand repurchase and subscription behaviour.
  • Defined a simple KPI set (revenue, cohorts, repeat rate, AutoShip adoption, churn).
  • Highlighted where the funnel was leaking and which segments were most likely to repeat.

Growth & Marketing Campaigns

  • Designed and executed marketing campaigns for acquisition and repeat purchases.
  • Supported automation journeys (onboarding, replenishment reminders, win-back flows).
  • Aligned messaging between campaigns, website and customer support so the journey felt coherent.

Brand Activation & Events

  • Collaborated on brand activation events in high-traffic locations to increase awareness and trust.
  • Connected offline events to digital journeys through specific offers and follow-up flows.

McDonald's Collaboration – Doggy Box

  • Contributed to the “Doggy Box” campaign with McDonald's, mixing physical experiences with online traffic and engagement.
  • Helped ensure the campaign translated into measurable demand, not just awareness.

Selected Milestones

  • Improved clarity on which segments were driving most of the recurring revenue.
  • Supported growth in repeat purchases and subscription adoption through clearer journeys.
  • Turned scattered data into simple, recurring views that the team could use every week.
B2B sales & supplier acquisition
Alisto · Marketplace connecting restaurants & suppliers
View case ↗

About Alisto — a B2B marketplace that connects restaurants with food & beverage suppliers, simplifying ordering for restaurants and giving suppliers a platform to manage their catalogue and reach new clients.

My role — I worked in the sales and supplier-side growth area, focusing on:

  • Acquiring and onboarding new suppliers across key categories.
  • Building and executing the supplier acquisition strategy.
  • Maintaining long-term relationships and consistent communication with active suppliers.
  • Aligning expectations between restaurants and suppliers to improve fulfilment and product availability.

Impact for restaurants

  • Centralised, structured ordering with multiple suppliers instead of scattered WhatsApp messages.
  • Faster comparison of prices, presentations and availability.
  • Clear historical record of orders and spending.

Impact for suppliers

  • New customer acquisition through marketplace visibility.
  • Simplified order management instead of improvised channels.
  • Better communication flow for stock changes, replacements and delivery coordination.

Data & operational improvements

  • Identified priority suppliers based on growth, fulfilment quality and order frequency.
  • Mapped operational bottlenecks where orders were getting delayed or lost.
  • Helped create a cleaner product catalogue with comparable presentations.

Customer & supplier experience

  • Clarified the end-to-end ordering flow for restaurant teams.
  • Set operational standards with suppliers for faster confirmation and fewer errors.
  • Improved communication loops between both sides of the marketplace.

Selected milestones

  • Successful onboarding of new suppliers into high-demand categories.
  • Higher order consistency from restaurants through better supplier coverage.
  • Reduced friction by shifting suppliers from ad-hoc communication to platform-based order handling.
Snapshot of Alisto — restaurants centralise orders, suppliers manage requests and discover new clients.
Founder · Product, strategy, sales & partnerships
Vitta Health · AI-powered medical automation platform
View case ↗

About Vitta Health — a healthtech platform that automates medical workflows using AI, helping doctors reduce documentation time, improve patient communication and streamline clinical tasks. I founded Vitta Health and led the product from idea to working platform.

  • AI-generated medical notes, summaries and clinical documents.
  • Smart triage and automated patient education.
  • Omnichannel communication (WhatsApp, web app, calls).
  • Predictive tools to identify demand peaks in medical services.

My role

  • Founder & product lead: Defined the product vision, structure, technical architecture and feature roadmap.
  • Strategy: Designed the go-to-market approach for doctors, clinics and health institutions.
  • Sales: Led B2B sales, demos and onboarding for early clinical users and partners.
  • Partnerships: Built and managed strategic relationships with health providers and organisations.

Key partnership

I led the partnership with Hospital Clínica Bíblica, the largest private hospital in Costa Rica, to explore innovation initiatives around AI-assisted care and digital medical workflows.

Impact areas

  • Reduced documentation time for doctors by automating repetitive tasks.
  • Improved patient experience with faster communication and clearer instructions.
  • Helped clinics scale by standardising medical documentation quality.
  • Introduced automated flows that simplified scheduling, follow-ups and clinical communication.

Selected milestones

  • Founded the project and built the first working version of the platform.
  • Secured early B2B clients and clinical users.
  • Established a strategic partnership with a major health institution.
  • Designed a roadmap focused on multi-speciality workflows and offline-first capability.
